Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you schedule a shoot?

Most projects can be scheduled within 3-7 days, weather permitting. Rush jobs may be accommodated based on availability.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Richmond, VA and surrounding areas within a 50-mile radius, including Henrico, Chesterfield, and Hanover counties.

Do you fly in all weather conditions?

Safety is our priority. We don't fly in rain, high winds, or poor visibility conditions. We'll reschedule if needed at no extra cost.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! We're FAA Part 107 certified and carry full commercial insurance for all drone operations.
